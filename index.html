A Young Woman of Many Hats

In the Life of Tia Dawson


Self Portrait of Tia

Magazine Cover of Tia

Careers

Tia Dawson is a young woman on the go. She lives life to the fullest.

For several years, Tia has been working two different jobs. One as a Special Event Coordinator for a local art gallery and a Sales Associate for a luxury shoe store. In addition, she is a model, fashion blogger and stylist.

Interests

Her favorite pass time is listening to music which she can do all day on any day. Tia started modeling when she was 16 and even has a cover of a national magazine. Her clothing style is mostly from the 70s.

Aspired Writer

She is a writer by nature and shares stories of life experiences on her blog. Writers have become so intrigues with her work that she was invited to attend writing classes facilitated by famous authors. Her goal is to write and publish a book one day.

Therapist by Nature

Tia does not meet strangers. In fact, she has been known to meet someone in a department store who notices something about her soul that makes want to share what is going on in their lives. This has been happening ever since Tia was very young. Because of her nature ability to provide counseling, she enrolled in the Psychology program when she went to college.

Natural Perfumer

She loves essential oils and has been known to formulate scents that people have loved so much when she passes by. Some of her favorites are nap champa, mask, jasmine and sandalwood.


Autisum Awareness

Recently, Tia discovered her godson is autistic. When given the opportunity to get involved, Tia jumped on the chance so that she can help out as much as possible with her godson and any one else.


Tia hopes to travel the world and make a difference while doing so.>
